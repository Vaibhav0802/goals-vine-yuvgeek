Tracks your goals, motivate and reminds you to convert them into reality.

What is Goals Vine?

Are you one of them who sets New Year resolution (or) any goals in the beginning of the year but you don't follow it after?

Then this is the app for you.

The reason why people lose focus is,

  • Forgetting the goals set due to busy life
  • No Motivation.
  • No way to Measure our goals

What Goals Vine offer?

Goals Vine solves the above problem by providing the following features.

  • Send you a reminder mail every month
  • Send Motivational quotes & images in reminder mail to continue your progress.
  • Offers statistics to show your growth
  • Categorize your goals to know how much you are achieving or lagging in specific category
  • Shows Leader board to compete with others
